About ThreadSync

Connecting craftsmanship with convenience

Our Story

ThreadSync was born from a simple observation: finding a skilled tailor shouldn't be difficult. In an age where everything is at our fingertips, connecting with quality craftsmen remained a challenge. We set out to change that.

Founded in 2020, ThreadSync has grown from a small startup to a thriving platform connecting thousands of customers with hundreds of verified tailors across the country. Our mission is to preserve and promote the art of tailoring while making it accessible to everyone.

Our Values

Quality First

We carefully vet every tailor on our platform to ensure exceptional craftsmanship.

Community

We support local artisans and help them grow their businesses.

Trust

Transparent pricing, verified reviews, and satisfaction guarantees.

Convenience

Easy booking, clear communication, and timely delivery.
