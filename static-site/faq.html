Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

General Questions

ThreadSync is a platform that connects customers with skilled tailors for custom clothing, alterations, and bespoke garments. We verify all tailors on our platform to ensure quality craftsmanship.
Simply browse our tailor directory, filter by specialty, location, or rating, and view detailed profiles including reviews and pricing. Once you find a tailor you like, you can book an appointment directly through our platform.
Yes, creating an account and browsing tailors is completely free. You only pay for the tailoring services you book. There are no hidden fees or subscription costs for customers.

Booking & Appointments

Select your preferred tailor, choose a service, pick an available date and time, and provide your contact details. You'll receive an instant confirmation via email and SMS.
Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment through your dashboard. We recommend giving at least 24 hours notice. Cancellation policies may vary by tailor.
For alterations, bring the garment(s) you need altered. For bespoke services, bring any reference images or fabric samples if you have specific preferences. Your tailor will guide you through the rest.

Pricing & Payment

Pricing varies by tailor and service type. All tailors display their starting prices on their profiles. Final pricing is determined during your consultation based on fabric choice, complexity, and customizations.
Payment methods vary by tailor. Most accept credit/debit cards, and some accept cash. Payment details are provided during the booking process.
Deposit requirements vary by tailor and service. For bespoke orders, a 50% deposit is common. Your tailor will discuss payment terms during your consultation.
